The Science Behind SubMax HIIT

Traditional HIIT often pushes you to your absolute limits, which can lead to burnout and increased injury risk. Our SubMax HIIT approach, based on scientific research, keeps your intensity in the optimal zone - high enough to drive improvements, but controlled enough to be sustainable.

This smarter approach allows for more frequent training sessions while reducing recovery time, leading to better long-term results. By maintaining your heart rate in the optimal training zone, you'll achieve:

  • Better fat burning efficiency
  • Improved cardiovascular endurance
  • Reduced risk of overtraining
  • More consistent progress over time
