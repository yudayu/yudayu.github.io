Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2024

Overview

Better HIIT is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices.

Data Collection

Better HIIT does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All your workout data and settings are stored locally on your device and are never sent to our servers or shared with third parties.

Health Data

The app accesses your heart rate data through Apple HealthKit solely for the purpose of providing real-time workout feedback. This data remains on your device and is not collected, stored, or transmitted by us.

Analytics

We do not use any analytics tools or tracking mechanisms. We have no way to track how you use the app or collect any usage statistics.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect any data, there is no personal information for you to access, modify, or delete. All your data remains under your complete control on your device.

Changes to This Policy

If we make any changes to this privacy policy, we will post the updated version here.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@betterhiit.app
